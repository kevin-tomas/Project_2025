The Document:

In April 2023, the Heritage Foundation published Project 2025, also known as the 2025 Presidential Transition Project. Meant to reshape the U.S. federal government by consolidating executive power under right-wing policies, the document provides a detailed roadmap for transforming the federal government and implementing conservative policies in a future Republican presidential administration.

One of the central pillars, an over 900 page policy document referred to as the "Mandate for Leadership 2025," outlines the restructuring of government departments, policies on immigration and borders, abortion rights, safeguards for LGBTQ+ people, law enforcement and policing, economic oversight, and public assistance programs. In total, consolidating power within the executive branch, and promoting traditional conservative ideology.
